Batman

Biography

Batman, also known as Bruce Wayne, is a wealthy businessman and philanthropist. After witnessing the murder of his parents, he dedicates his life to fighting crime in Gotham City using his intellect, detective skills, and physical prowess.

Abilities

  • Intelligence
  • Combat Skills
  • Unlimited Wealth
  • Peak Human Condition
  • Stealth and Agility

First Appearance

Batman first appeared in Detective Comics #27 in 1939

  • Determined
  • Justice-driven
